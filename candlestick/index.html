Candlestick

Summary

A candlestick chart is a type of financial chart used to show price movements of a security, derivative, or currency. One candle represents a single day with the information for the high, low, open, and close. Candlestick charts are often used for representing stock trading patterns and information.

Analysis

Candlestick charts use lines and areas as marks. The top line of the candlestick represents the high for the day, while the bottom line represents the low.
